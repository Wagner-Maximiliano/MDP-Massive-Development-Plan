# MDP Role Model

MDP defines generalized roles first. Specific agents are mapped into those roles per environment.

## Roles

### Human Owner
Final authority. Gives goals, approves major uncertain choices, and can override all roles.

### PA / Orchestrator
Intake, routing, momentum, summaries, delegation, handovers, and human communication.

### COO / Decision Coordinator
Strategic and operational decisions, confidence scoring, escalation, project priority, risk balance.

### CTO / Technical Lead
Architecture, engineering quality, implementation strategy, technical review, deployment, automation.

### CFO / Finance Lead
Budget, pricing, subscriptions, model/API cost, investment, financial tradeoffs.

### Operator / Utility Agent
Bounded support work: summaries, formatting, data cleanup, structured scans, repetitive workflows.

### Specialist Workers
Scoped execution under a lead role. They receive narrow tasks, boundaries, expected output, and verification requirements.
